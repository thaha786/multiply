Attitude is Altitude

He was born whit rare disorder without arms and legs. His mother refused to even look at him while the nurse held the baby. On his birth, his dad nearly fainted and vomited so the nurse had to take him out of the room.

Not only did he have troubles with everyday tasks, but he was also ridiculed and bullied by other students at school. The kids laughed at him and excluded from every game.

He was lonely, depressed and thought that he had no reason to live in the world.

At 10, the continuous depression led him to commit suicide. He felt, that he had no value and that he would always be a burden to his parents.

One day his mother showed him a man in a newspaper, who was suffering from severe disability too. It made him realise, that he was not alone in the world with this conditions. He started to accept himself the way he was. He began using his toes to write, swim and play.

He travelled the world and inspired the life of millions with his motivational talks. Today Nick Vujicic is a world famous motivational speaker.
